How to read this & what's a "phantom"

✓ Verified (bookable): business seats + a real price + every carrier is one the booking program can actually ticket. The list on the first tab is all verified.
Unpriced ($0): seats shown, no price captured. Usually not bookable in business — except Delta, where it's just seats.aero failing to price (those nonstops are real, schedules confirmed).
Phantom — you canNOT book it: seats.aero stitched two pieces of availability into one award the program can't issue. The clearest example: a 75k Aeroplan return of AZ576 (ITA Airways = SkyTeam) + LX22 (Swiss = Star Alliance). Aeroplan is Star-only — it can't put ITA on the same ticket, so that 75k price isn't real. I auto-flagged every one of these.
Mixed cabin: on 1-stop European routings the transatlantic leg is true lie-flat; the short connector (CDG/AMS) is "Euro-business" (recliner, blocked middle). Normal.
Two bookings: outbound and return are separate awards. Flying Blue miles transfer from Amex / Chase / Citi / Cap One / Bilt.
